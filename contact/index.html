Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide mobile automotive service throughout South Florida, including all of Broward County (Fort Lauderdale, Plantation, Pembroke Pines, Hollywood, Coral Springs, Pompano Beach) and Palm Beach County (West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach). Call us to confirm we service your specific location.

How does mobile service work?

Simply call or text us with your vehicle's needs and location. We'll schedule an appointment at a time that works for you. Our fully equipped mobile service van arrives at your home, office, or wherever your vehicle is located. We perform the service on-site, so you don't waste time driving to a shop or waiting in a lobby.

Is mobile service more expensive than a shop?

Our rates are competitive with traditional repair shops. While there may be a small trip charge for some locations, you save time, avoid towing fees, and don't lose productivity sitting in a waiting room. Most customers find mobile service to be excellent value considering the convenience and time saved.

Do you work on all vehicle makes and models?

Yes, we service most domestic, European, and Asian vehicles. Whether you drive a Honda, Toyota, Ford, Chevrolet, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, or any other make, we have the expertise and tools to maintain and repair it properly. If you have an unusual or exotic vehicle, call us to confirm we can service it.

What services can be done mobile vs. at a shop?

Most common maintenance and repair services can be performed on-site: oil changes, brake service, battery replacement, starter/alternator replacement, diagnostics, serpentine belts, thermostats, tie rod ends, shock absorbers, spark plugs, and more. Services requiring specialized equipment like wheel alignments, tire mounting, or major transmission work may need to be done at a shop. We'll let you know upfront if a service requires shop equipment.

Do you provide parts or do I need to buy them?

We provide all necessary parts and fluids for the service. We use quality OEM or premium aftermarket parts matched to your vehicle's specifications. If you prefer to supply your own parts, let us know in advance and we can discuss options.

Are your services documented?

Absolutely. All completed services are professionally documented and added to your vehicle's Carfax history. This maintains your vehicle's resale value, provides proof of maintenance, and gives you a clear service record. We run our mobile business with the same professionalism as any established shop.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and digital payment methods. Payment is due upon completion of service. We'll provide you with a detailed invoice showing all work performed and parts used.

How quickly can you come out?

In many cases, we can provide same-day or next-day service. For urgent issues like a vehicle that won't start or is overheating, we'll do our best to come out as quickly as possible. For routine maintenance, we can usually accommodate your preferred schedule within a few days.

Do you offer any warranties?

Yes, we stand behind our work. Most services come with a parts and labor warranty. Warranty terms vary by service and parts used—we'll explain the specific warranty for your service before we begin work. If you experience any issues after service, contact us immediately and we'll make it right.
