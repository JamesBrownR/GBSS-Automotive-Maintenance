Common AC Problems

Warm Air: Low refrigerant, compressor failure, or blend door issues

Weak Airflow: Clogged cabin filter, blower motor problems, or duct obstructions

Strange Noises: Compressor bearing failure or debris in blower motor

Bad Odors: Moldy evaporator, clogged drain, or contaminated cabin filter

Signs You Need AC Service

Weak Airflow

Little to no air coming from vents

Warm Air

AC blowing warm or hot air

Strange Sounds

Rattling, squealing, or clicking noises

Water Leaks

Pooling water inside vehicle
