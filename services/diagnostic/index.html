Professional Vehicle Diagnostics

Modern vehicles are equipped with sophisticated computer systems that monitor hundreds of sensors and components. When something goes wrong, your check engine light illuminates. Our professional diagnostic service uses advanced scan tools to read trouble codes, identify issues, and determine the root cause of problems before recommending repairs.

What We Diagnose:

Check Engine Light: Read and interpret OBD-II trouble codes

ABS/Brake System: Anti-lock brake and traction control diagnostics

Airbag System: SRS/airbag warning light diagnostics

Transmission: Automatic transmission codes and issues

Engine Performance: Misfires, rough idle, stalling, power loss

Emissions: O2 sensors, catalytic converter, EVAP system

Electrical: Battery, alternator, starting/charging systems

Our Diagnostic Process

1. Initial Consultation: We discuss your vehicle's symptoms and concerns

2. Computer Scan: We connect to your vehicle's computer system

3. Code Analysis: We interpret trouble codes and live data

4. Physical Inspection: We verify findings with hands-on testing

5. Detailed Report: We explain the problem and recommend solutions

Common Warning Signs

Don't ignore these symptoms – schedule a diagnostic today:

Check engine light on
Rough or irregular idle
Reduced fuel economy
Hard starting or stalling
Loss of power
Strange noises or odors
