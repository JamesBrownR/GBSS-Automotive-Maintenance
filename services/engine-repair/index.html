Oil Leaks: Diagnose and repair all engine oil leaks

Engine Mounts: Reduce vibration and noise

Intake & Exhaust: Manifold, gasket, and sensor repairs

Warning Signs of Engine Problems

Check engine light illuminated

Engine knocking or ticking sounds

Rough idle or stalling

Loss of power or acceleration

Excessive exhaust smoke (blue, white, or black)

Oil or coolant leaks under vehicle

Overheating or temperature gauge in red zone
