Tire Rotation Service

Extend tire life and improve vehicle handling with regular tire rotation

Why Tire Rotation Matters

Tires wear unevenly depending on their position on your vehicle. Front tires typically wear faster due to steering and braking forces, while rear tires wear differently based on drive type. Regular tire rotation ensures even wear across all four tires, maximizing their lifespan, improving handling, and maintaining safe traction.

Our Tire Rotation Service Includes:

Remove and rotate all four tires according to manufacturer pattern

Inspect tread depth and tire condition

Check tire pressure and adjust to proper PSI

Inspect for cuts, bulges, or uneven wear patterns

Torque lug nuts to manufacturer specifications

Service documented in Carfax

Recommended Rotation Interval

Every 5,000-7,500 miles or every oil change
Regular rotation can extend tire life by 20% or more, saving you money in the long run. We recommend rotating tires with every oil change for convenience.

Signs You Need Tire Rotation

It's been more than 7,500 miles since your last rotation

You notice uneven tread wear between front and rear tires

Your vehicle pulls to one side while driving

You feel vibration through the steering wheel at highway speeds
